It's Anna's Very Special Birthday Day!

So how about we play some trivia to celebrate? What a fun night we will have!

Let's Play!

How does it work?

First, we will break out into teams so everyone can introduce themselves to one another. During this time, come up with a team name, and designate one person on the team to be the designated form filler outer. Also, take a screenshot of your team on Zoom to send to us later!

For the trivia itself, we will have 4 rounds, each with a different theme. We will read the questions, and then send each team out into breakout rooms to discuss and decide on answers. You may want to have a pen and paper handy to jot down ideas while we are reading the questions (this will be especially true for the music round). The answer forms for each round can be found below. You have 10 minutes!

We will grade the answers, and the scores will show up in the table below. The winning team will each receive $15 UberEats gift cards. Second place gets a virtual medal. Third place gets virtual pride.

On to the game!
